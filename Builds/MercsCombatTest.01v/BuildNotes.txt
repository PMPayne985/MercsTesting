Pre-alpha build v 0.01: Endurance pool test

Controls:
WASD - basic movement
Mouse Axis - character rotation
Left Mouse Button/Left Ctrl - kill an enemy withing 2.5 meters
Left Shift - spend 2 Endurance to increase speed by 60% for 4 seconds
Left Alt - spend 2 Endurance for a 75% chance to reduce falling damage by 2 (min 0 damage)
ESC - open or close the menu

Enemies will deal 1-4 endurance damage to the player when within 3 meters ever 1 second. When a player is out of endurance, enemies will deal 1 wounds damage instead. This damage is dealt 100% of the time reguardless of facing.

Build will test basics of shared ability/damage pool.
